Student Kiosk

How to

  1. Create an Account
  2. First time? "Sign Up" to create an account. Once your account is created, log in using your email and password.
  3. Explore the kiosk’s menu within the app. You can navigate through categories like snacks, beverages, or other items available for purchase.
  4. Add items to your cart by selecting them and tapping "+" and remove by typing "-".
  5. When you’ve selected all the items you want, tap on your cart icon to review your order.
  6. Make sure everything is correct, then tap "Checkout".
  7. Select Payment Method
  8. Pick Up Your Items
